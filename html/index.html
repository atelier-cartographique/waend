wænd

wænd is a web platform dedicated to spatial publication, fragmental cartography, and collaborative mapping. This application is open source, free to use, and under constant development.

Developing long term research within the field of territorial narratives & representations, we collaborate with artists, universities, researchers, and citizen driven organisations.
